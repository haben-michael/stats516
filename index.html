Description (from catalogue): Continuation of Stat 515. Overall objective of the course is the development of basic theory and methods for statistical inference. Topics include: Sampling distributions; General techniques for statistical inference (point estimation, confidence intervals, tests of hypotheses); Development of methods for inferences on one or more means (one-sample, two-sample, many samples - one-way analysis of variance), inference on proportions (including contingency tables), simple linear regression and non-parametric methods (time permitting).
Prerequisites: Stat 515 or 515H with a grade of "C" or better.
Textbook: Wackerly, Mendenhall, Schaeffer, Mathematical Statistics with Applications, 7th Ed. (ISBN-13: 978-0495110811). [Library reserve TBA]
